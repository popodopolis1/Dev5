---Project Controls---

-Camera-
W,A,S,D = Standard Camera Movement
Left Click = Rotate Camera
Space = Move Camera Straight Up
Left Shift = Move Camera Straight Down

-Rendering-
Q = Turn Wireframe Mesh On and Off
E = Turn Solid Mesh On and Off
Up,Down,Left,Right Arrows = Moves Light

-Animation-
N = Keyframe press
L = Looping animation
